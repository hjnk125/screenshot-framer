How to Create a Device Mockup

  1. Upload your screenshot (PNG or JPG, up to 4000 px).
  2. Pick a device frame (iPhone, MacBook, iMac) or a browser frame (Chrome, Safari).
  3. Adjust scale, pan, and shadow to your liking.
  4. Click Export PNG to download your mock-up.
